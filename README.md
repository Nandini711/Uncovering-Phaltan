# 🏛️ Uncovering Phaltan

A comprehensive website showcasing the rich history and cultural heritage of Phaltan city, along with detailed information about tourist attractions both within the city and in nearby regions.

## 📖 Project Overview

This project provides an in-depth exploration of:
- **Historical Background**: The fascinating history of Phaltan city
- **Tourist Attractions**: Popular places to visit in Phaltan
- **Nearby Destinations**: Recommended tourist spots in surrounding areas
- **Cultural Information**: Local culture, heritage, and landmarks

## 🎯 Features

- Comprehensive historical timeline
- Detailed tourist destination guide
- Interactive location mapping
- Information about famous monuments and temples
- Travel recommendations for nearby areas
- Cultural and heritage information

## 🗺️ About Phaltan

Phaltan is a historic city known for its cultural significance and heritage sites. This website serves as your digital guide to exploring the city's rich past and vibrant present.
